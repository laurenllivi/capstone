Square Footage Comparisons

Don't know how to estimate your space's square footage?
Maybe these comparisons will help:

  • The interior of a school bus is about 300 square feet.
  • The average 3-car garage is a little over 600 square feet
  • A regulation-sized basketball court is about 4,700 square feet
  • An NHL ice rink measures 20,000 square feet.
  • A football field (including endzones) measures about 57,000 square feet
